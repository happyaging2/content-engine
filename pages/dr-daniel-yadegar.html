About

Dr. Daniel Yadegar is a board-certified cardiologist (FACC) and Registered Physician in Vascular Interpretation (RPVI). He serves as the medical reviewer for Happy Aging's editorial content, evaluating every article for clinical accuracy, evidence quality, and adherence to FDA / FTC guidelines for dietary-supplement communication.

Dr. Yadegar's clinical focus spans cardiovascular medicine, vascular health, and longevity-oriented preventive care for adults navigating midlife and beyond.

Editorial role at Happy Aging

Every Happy Aging article that addresses a health topic is reviewed by Dr. Yadegar before publication. His review covers:

  • Clinical accuracy of every claim
  • Verification that all citations resolve to real peer-reviewed studies (PMID/DOI)
  • FDA compliance: no claims that a supplement diagnoses, treats, cures, or prevents disease
  • FTC compliance: every objective claim is substantiated by competent and reliable scientific evidence
  • Appropriate hedging language, disclaimers, and special-population safety call-outs
