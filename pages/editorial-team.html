The Happy Aging Editorial Team

Happy Aging articles are written by a small team of women's-health writers and longevity researchers, and every piece is medically reviewed by Dr. Daniel Yadegar, MD, FACC, RPVI, board-certified cardiologist and longevity physician.

Editorial standards

  • Every numeric claim is sourced to peer-reviewed research with a PMID or DOI.
  • We do not cite blogs, press releases, or manufacturer marketing as evidence.
  • Articles are written in US English at a 6th-8th grade reading level.
  • Every article is reviewed by Dr. Yadegar before publication and re-reviewed every 90 days.
  • We disclose third-party clinical testing (Citrus Labs) where relevant.
