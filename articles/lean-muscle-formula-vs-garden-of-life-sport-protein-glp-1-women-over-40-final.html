If you are on a GLP-1 medication and trying to protect your muscle mass, the protein supplement you choose matters more than it does for anyone else. What the Research Says

Current evidence supports protein intake at 1.2 to 1.6 grams per kilogram body weight daily combined with resistance training as the most effective strategy for maintaining lean mass during GLP-1 treatment. A 2023 analysis found that participants who maintained higher protein intake and continued resistance training retained significantly more lean mass during semaglutide treatment.

According to Happy Aging's review, women on GLP-1 medications should treat protein supplementation as a clinical tool. Every gram counts when total calorie intake is suppressed.

The Happy Aging Recommendation

  1. Take one serving of Lean Muscle Formula within 30 minutes of resistance training on workout days.
  2. On non-training days, take as a between-meal supplement to support daily protein targets.
  3. Aim for 1.2 to 1.6 grams of protein per kilogram of body weight daily across meals and supplements.
  4. Pair with at least 2 to 3 resistance training sessions per week.
  5. If appetite is suppressed, a smaller serving of a concentrated, leucine-rich formula is more practical than a large shake.
