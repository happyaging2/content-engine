What the Research Says

Both products depend on lion's mane. Human trials (Mori et al. 2009 RCT, Saitsu et al. 2020) show modest but significant improvements in memory and cognitive function. MCT oil has solid evidence for acute cognitive support, particularly fasted. A 2008 study (Haskell et al., Biological Psychology) found that L-theanine combined with caffeine improved attention-switching and reduced distraction.

According to Happy Aging's review, the Neuro Creamer's combination of lion's mane plus MCT oil plus L-theanine addresses three distinct cognitive support mechanisms. Four Sigmatic's strength is in mushroom sourcing quality and chaga for broad antioxidant support.
